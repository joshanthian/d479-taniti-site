By Cruise

Some travelers arrive on a small cruise ship that docks at Yellow Leaf Bay for one night each week.

Public Buses

Public buses run in Taniti City daily from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. Private buses also serve the rest of the island.

Taxis

Taxi service is available throughout Taniti City for quick and convenient local travel.

Rental Cars

Visitors can rent vehicles near the airport through a local car rental agency.

Bikes

Bikes and helmets are available for rent from multiple vendors. Taniti City is flat and easily walkable for tourists.

Note: Helmets are required by law when biking in Taniti.
